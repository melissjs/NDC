Thank you for participating in the Democracy Counts! 2016 General Election Audit. This process takes about three minutes; there are three parts to fill out:

1) An affidavit
2) Your vote and voter preferences
3) Demographic information

The objective of this independent, non-profit and volnteer organized audit is to safegaurd election inegrity by providing data that will be compared with the official recorded vote share at this polling station. If this audit uncovers substantial vote share discrepencies, our data will be instrumental in potential legal proceedings.

*Your personal information is encrypted, kept private and stored separately from your vote record. Your vote will never be linked to your personal information.
